About Me

I am Erik Li, a researcher interested in multimodal large language models, explainable AI, and reasoning. My research focuses on understanding how large models process visual and textual information, how they reason, and how we can improve their transparency and reliability.

I am particularly interested in interpretability, attention analysis, hallucination mitigation, and trustworthy multimodal systems.

You can find my code and projects on GitHub.
