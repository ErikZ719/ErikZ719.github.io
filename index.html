About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Xu-Yao Zhang. Previously, I was a research intern at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I had the privilege of collaborating with and learning from Xiaofeng Zhang. My research focuses on explainable AI and multimodal large language models (MLLMs).

My current interests lie in multimodal reasoning and embodied intelligence (VLA). Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in related topics.
